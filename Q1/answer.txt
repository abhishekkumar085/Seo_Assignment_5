Q:What is SEO and Explain the Importance of seo?
Ans:SEO, which stands for Search Engine Optimization, is a set of practices and techniques aimed at improving a website's visibility and ranking on search engine results pages . The primary goal of SEO is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to a website by making it more attractive and relevant to search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo.

Importance of seo:-
1.Increases Visibility
2.Attracts Relevant Users and Expands Your Reach
3.Increase Web Traffic
4.Credibility and Trust
5.Cost-Effective
6.Long-Term Strategy
